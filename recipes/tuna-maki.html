Tuna Maki Roll

Tuna Maki Roll, also called Tekka is a simple and delicios sushi roll.

Ingrediants

  • Sushi Grade Tuna
  • Nori
  • Sushi Rice
  • Wasabi
  • Soy Sauce
  • Bowl of water

Instructions

  1. Wet your hands in the bowl of water before picking up the rice.
  2. Spread sushi rice out on Nori, rough side up.
  3. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top of the rice.
  4. Smear Wasabi horizontally along rice.
  5. Lay the Tuna down.
  6. Roll the nori over itself with a maki mat.
  7. Slice the sushi into eight pieces.
  8. Dip the maki roll into the soy sauce, and enjoy.
