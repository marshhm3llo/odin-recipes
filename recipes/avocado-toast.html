Avocado Toast

Avocado Toast is a tasty and easy breakfast or lunch food.

Ingrediants

  • Avocado
  • Bread
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Lemon

Instructions

  1. First, insert your bread in to the toaster.
  2. Second, slice the avocado into thin layers.
  3. Then you'll add the avocado on top of the toast.
  4. Season the toast with salt and pepper.
  5. Squeeze some lemon on top, and the toast is ready to be enjoyed.
