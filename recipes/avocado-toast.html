Avocado Toast

Avocado Toast is tasty and easy breakfast or lunch plate.

Ingrediants

  • Avocado
  • Bread
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Tomato
  • Spinach
  • Onion
  • Lemon

Instructions

  • First, put your toast in the toaster.
  • Second, slice the avocado into thin layers.
  • Then you'll add the avocado on top of the toast.
  • Season the toast with salt and pepper.
  • Next, add the vegetables on top.
  • Squeeze some lemon on top, and the toast is ready to be enjoyed.
