Shrimp Scampi

Shrimp scampi is a delicious Italian dish.

It consists of Garlic and butter nooldes with shrimp.

Ingrediants

  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Linguine
  • Shrimp
  • chili flakes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • White wine
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Basil

Instructions

  1. First, heat a pan with butter.
  2. Next, add minced garlic.
  3. Then add the shrimp, with a couple dashes of salt and pepper.
  4. Once the shrimp have cooked 2 minutes on each side, add wine and let simmer.
  5. Next, toss the noodles in with the shrimp.
  6. Add parmesan cheese and stir thoroughly.
  7. Last but not least, add sliced basil, and serve hot.
